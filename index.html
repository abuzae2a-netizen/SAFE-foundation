Our Mission

We are committed to building safe communities through five core pillars of action.

Safe Haven

Providing safe shelter and care for vulnerable, homeless, and orphaned children through our Bait Noor project and family support programs.

Education

Supporting girl child education, literacy programs, and vocational training for youth and women to break cycles of poverty.

Climate Action

Promoting eco-friendly practices, tree planting, and green initiatives to build a resilient and sustainable future for our communities.

Community Development

Strengthening families through marriage counselling, parent-child dialogue, community watch, and neighbourhood cleanup initiatives.

Good Governance

Promoting transparency, accountability, and civic participation through election observation, FOI advocacy, and cross-party oversight.

SAFE Council

Our governance and election observation arm — uniting delegates from all political parties, universities, and economic sectors.

  • Monitor government from local to federal level
  • Cross-party oversight — no bias, no partisanship
  • Deploy trained election observers (INEC accredited)
  • House-to-house civic education and voter mobilisation
  • Public scorecards and FOI advocacy for accountability
