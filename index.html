About Us

SAFE Foundation is a non-profit, grassroots organization dedicated to creating opportunities, restoring hope, and building stronger communities across Northern Nigeria.

SAFE Council: Our Governance & Civic Engagement Arm

SAFE Council is a citizen-led initiative, operating under the SAFE Foundation umbrella, that brings together Nigerians from all walks of life – political parties, universities, businesses, and communities – to support our government in delivering better results for our people.

We believe that when citizens and government work together, we build stronger communities and a more prosperous nation. Our role is to provide constructive, evidence-based feedback that helps public officials identify what's working, address challenges, and continuously improve service delivery.

We are not an opposition group. We are partners in national development – committed to ensuring that public resources are managed effectively, policies are implemented efficiently, and every Nigerian feels the impact of good governance in their daily lives.

Our Mission Areas

Community Development

Strengthening families through marriage counselling, parent-child dialogue, community watch, neighbourhood cleanup, and family sensitization initiatives.

Good Governance

Promoting transparency, accountability, and civic participation through election observation, FOI advocacy, and cross-party oversight.

Our Projects

High-value, long-term projects that create lasting change in our communities.

Rotating safety patrols, neighbourhood cleanup, and community development initiatives.

Election Observation

Good Governance
